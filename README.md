Google Account app passwords are 16-digit codes allowing less secure, non-Google apps (e.g., older email clients, SMTP tools) to access your account. They require 2-Step Verification to be enabled. Generate them at https://myaccount.google.com/apppasswords by naming the app/device and copying the generated code, which replaces your standard password for that specific application.
